Fill in Master Data

  •   Master Data includes Vendor, Products & Customers.

  •   User registration module and fill in the form.

Making Purchases & Stock In

  •    Go to [Purchase – Purchase Order] choose which Vendor to purchase and fill in all required information’s

  •    After PO form is generated, need to be approved by Admin from Admin’s reminders

  •    After PO is approved, need to generate invoice from [Invoice – Purchase Invoice] and choose the correct IPO number

  •    From [Purchase – Purchase View] user can check the purchase delivery status and payment status (still pending now)

  •    To change payment status to PAID, go to [Payment – Payable] select the correct IPO number and input the payment that has been made (full payment/partial)

  •    To change delivery status to DELIVERED, go to [Purchase – Make Delivery] select the correct IPO number and click action button

  •    Each PO will be closed when the status is Paid & Delivered

  •    After the PO is delivered, we need to input the purchases to stock through [Stocks – Stock in] and select the correct IPO number

Making Sales Order

  •    Go to [Sales – Sales Order] and choose customer to sell to, fill in the form

  •    After sales order is made, we can check through [Sales – Sales View] for delivery & payment status (still pending)

  •    Before receiving payment, need to generate invoice through [Invoice – Sales Invoice] and select the correct SON number

  •    To change payment status go to [Payment – Receivable] choose the correct SON number and input the Received payment (full/partial)

  •    To change delivery status go to [Sales – Make Delivery] choose the correct SON number and click action button

  •    Every Sales made automatically deduct the Stock

  •    If any customer came to return their Sales, can use [Sales – Sales Return] to process

  •    For walk in customers, can use [Sales – Quick Sales] to quickly generate Sales Invoice

View Reports

  •    User can check daily/weekly/monthly Sales and Purchase report through [Report] module

  •   For Profit & Loss Statement can be viewed through [Accounts – Profit & Loss]

Register & Create Payroll for Employee

  •    To register employee data, use [Accounts – Payroll – Employee Registration] and fill all required informations

  •   To input payroll data (bonus, late charge and etc) use [Accounts – Payroll – Payroll form]

  •   To generate payroll slip use [Accounts – Payroll – Payroll View]

Input Transaction (Income/Expenses)

  •    To input income or expense outside Purchases & Sales user can go to [Accounts – Transaction Form] and fill in the required informations
